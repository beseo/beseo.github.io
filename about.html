About Me


Hello! My name is Beomsuk Seo. I am a student at the University of California, San Diego,
currently working towards my B.S. in Data Science at the Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute.
Currently in my 2nd year (Junior Standing), I am on track to complete my degree by the
Spring of 2025. As of right now, I am seeking an internship for the Summer of 2023.

I was born in Daegu, South Korea (2003) and have lived in Los Angeles since moving
here at 3 years old. Since then, I have found my love for coding, music, and group dynamics.
I've never liked wasting time, and still am quite picky about where I allocate my efforts. The
things I do spend my time on, however, I give my absolute all to, leading me to be known as
a sort of "Jack of a few trades, master of all" between my colleagues.

In my free time, I like to play the Cello and Piano, study Japanese, and sing. All of my
passions have led me to develop meaningful relationships with others, and I hope to
continue forming new bonds, and grow with those around me.
